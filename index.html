StartupSoftball is Toronto's first and only softball tournament for the tech community.

For our innagural tournament, we're taking over Cornation Park on Saturday, July 23rd to bring Toronto's startup community together for a hackathon of homeruns and high fives. Gather your team of 11 (or more) and get ready to put in some sweat equity, because there's no bandwidth in baseball.
